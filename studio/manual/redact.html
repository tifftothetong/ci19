HOW
            to watch a movie from
         “My List”
                  on netflix

                                                                  :
                                                                      a manual

Firstly, log into Netflix by accessing netflix.com

Secondly, select user

Thirdly, go to the top band/ row of the page and click on “My List”.

Fourthly, scroll and browse through the section of TV shows and films.

Fifth, when decided, click on the film you want to see. It should automatically move to fullscreen viewing mode.

Sixth, grab some snacks and enjoy your movie!
